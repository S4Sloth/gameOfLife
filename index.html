The Game of Life

Welcome to The Game Of Life!

If you are somehow new to the concept of living - your main goal is to survive! To survive you need to wisely manage this stats:

Money

You earn money by working and can spend it on necessary and not so necessary things.

Health

Tip: this should be your main focus, this points is very easy to lose and hard to get. If you reach 0, you will die.

Morale

Shows joy in your life. Stressful jobs and illnesses decreases morale, interesting hobbies and nice items - increases it.

Intelligence

The closer you are to 100, the cooler job opportunites will open to you. Be careful - being smart has disadvantages.

Prestige

The higher the stat, the more you are trendy, fashionable and hip. Only stat that is NOT limited, you can increases it as high as you want.

Energy

Your energy for doing additional activities besides job and hobby. It resets every month.
